LinkedIn Backgrounds

During my internship at GH Phipps Construction Company, I was tasked with improving employee LinkedIn profiles since they are connected to the company. I created LinkedIn backgrounds on Photoshop which featured various projects the company worked on. I then sent individuals their headshot along with these backgrounds. This project added a more cohesive look to GH Phipps employees who chose to update their profile.
